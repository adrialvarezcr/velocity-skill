# VELOCITY Frameworks Master Index

108 core concepts, organized by type and function. Each framework appears with its source essay(s) and primary use case.

## Execution Frameworks (Do This)

### Lanzar Imperfecto Construye Imperios
**When:** You're waiting for perfection before shipping.
**Action:** Ship 70% done. The market will teach you what 100% looks like faster than your overthinking will.
**Principle:** Velocity compounds. Perfection delays.

### Tirar la Escalera (Burn Your Escape Routes)
**When:** You keep one foot in the old world while building the new one.
**Action:** Remove your fallback options. Commitment forces creativity.
**Principle:** Half-commitment kills ventures faster than honest failure does.

### Cruzar Umbrales
**When:** A phase-change moment arrives and you don't move through it.
**Action:** These moments don't repeat. Cross or regret forever.
**Principle:** Timing is real. Some doors close permanently.

### Movimientos que Transforman
**When:** You're taking actions but nothing is changing structurally.
**Action:** Not all moves are equal. Some shift the game board itself.
**Principle:** Moves that transform are rare. Recognize them when they arrive.

### Perfecciona Ejecutando
**When:** Perfection paralyzes you (see: Lanzar Imperfecto).
**Action:** Perfect by doing, not by planning.
**Principle:** Each execution round teaches more than a year of refinement.

### Lead Magnets que Matan Listas
**When:** Your lead magnet isn't actually creating demand.
**Action:** Lead magnets don't build empires—they build audiences. Know which you need.
**Principle:** Clarity about what you're building matters more than complexity of your funnel.

## Mindset Frameworks (Think This)

### Ambición sin Culpa
**When:** You're diminishing your ambition to fit in or please others.
**Action:** Ambitious people build things. Build without apologizing.
**Principle:** Your ambition isn't greedy—it's generative.

### Tus Cicatrices son Credenciales
**When:** You hide your failures and struggles.
**Action:** Your scars are the evidence you've been in the arena.
**Principle:** Vulnerability becomes authority when paired with results.

### Tu Sombra te Delata
**When:** Your behavior doesn't match your values.
**Action:** The gap between what you claim and what you do is your real architecture.
**Principle:** You can't fake competence or character for long. Shadow work first.

### Quitarse la Máscara
**When:** You're performing a version of yourself that isn't real.
**Action:** The cost of the mask exceeds its value past a certain scale.
**Principle:** Authenticity scales better than performance.

### Liderazgo Invisible
**When:** You think leadership is about visibility and credit-taking.
**Action:** The best leaders make others powerful, not themselves big.
**Principle:** Invisible influence > visible authority.

### Lo que Haces Habla
**When:** Words matter less than they used to.
**Action:** Action is language now.
**Principle:** Your moves are your message.

## Decision Frameworks (Choose This Way)

### Elegir vs Decidir
**When:** You're confusing choosing (picking) with deciding (committing).
**Action:** Deciding includes removing your escape routes. Choosing leaves them open.
**Principle:** Commitment creates resourcefulness that choice never does.

### Cambio de Fase
**When:** The old rules stopped working but you haven't adapted.
**Action:** Phase changes require new mental models and new moves.
**Principle:** What worked in phase 1 usually kills phase 2.

### Los que Crean vs Observan
**When:** You're waiting for perfect information before acting.
**Action:** Creators have incomplete information. Observers never do.
**Principle:** All information is incomplete. Creators move anyway.

### Activa no Busques
**When:** You're researching instead of trying.
**Action:** Activation teaches. Research delays.
**Principle:** Move first, understand second.

### Permiso Retroactivo
**When:** You're waiting for permission to start.
**Action:** You don't ask for permission. You act, and if it works, permission follows.
**Principle:** Authority is given by results, not granted in advance.

## Anti-Patterns (Avoid This)

### La Falacia del Cartógrafo
**When:** You spend time mapping instead of exploring.
**Action:** Maps aren't territories. Dragons on maps led to discovery.
**Principle:** Some knowledge only exists in unexplored space. You have to move through it.

### La Ilusión de Competencia
**When:** You feel skilled because you understand something intellectually.
**Action:** Competence requires repetition. Understanding requires only thought.
**Principle:** The gap between knowing and doing is the entire game.

### Por Qué te Quedas Atrapado Donde Crees que Triunfas
**When:** Your early success becomes your ceiling.
**Action:** Success in one context blinds you to new ones.
**Principle:** What made you win before usually prevents you from winning next.

### Ciego a la Oportunidad
**When:** Opportunity passes because you're not looking for it.
**Action:** You can't see what you're not prepared to see.
**Principle:** Preparation is the only way to make luck visible.

### Donde Todos Piensan Igual
**When:** You're in rooms where everyone agrees.
**Action:** Agreement is a sign you're not exploring new territory.
**Principle:** Breakthroughs happen at the edges, not in the center.

### La Comodidad Adormece
**When:** Things feel comfortable and you mistake it for winning.
**Action:** Comfort is entropy. It requires active energy to maintain growth.
**Principle:** Discomfort is information. Comfort is a trap.

### Parálisis Disfrazada
**When:** You're busy but not moving.
**Action:** Activity and progress are not the same.
**Principle:** Some moves look productive but create zero compounding.

## Laws & Effects

### Efecto Zajonc
**When:** You confuse exposure with conversion.
**Action:** Familiarity increases preference—but you need to show up repeatedly.
**Principle:** Repetition creates comfort, but only if you're actually in the room.

### Efecto de Sobrecompensación
**When:** You overcorrect after a failure.
**Action:** The rebound swing is often larger than the original failure.
**Principle:** Know your patterns so you can compensate consciously.

### El Efecto Green Lumber
**When:** You confuse what you know with what matters.
**Action:** The best investors often focus on what others call boring or basic.
**Principle:** Simple usually beats complex. Focus beats scattered.

### Ley de Amara
**When:** You overestimate what's possible in 1 year, underestimate what's possible in 10.
**Action:** Compound time beats intensity.
**Principle:** The long game has different rules.

### Ley de Brandolini
**When:** Bullshit takes 1 unit of energy to create, 100 units to refute.
**Action:** Don't waste energy on noise.
**Principle:** Let wrong ideas die from neglect.

### Paradoja de Easterlin
**When:** You chase money thinking it will make you happy.
**Action:** Happiness and wealth are weakly correlated after basic needs are met.
**Principle:** The hedonic treadmill is real. Choose differently.

## Strategic Frameworks

### Tu Propósito Estructural
**When:** You're doing things but not building toward something.
**Action:** Purpose gives shape to random actions.
**Principle:** Direction matters more than speed.

### La Paradoja de Teseo
**When:** You're changing so much that your original vision disappears.
**Action:** Transformation is necessary. Loss is inevitable.
**Principle:** Build something worthy of changing.

### Dragones en el Mapa
**When:** The unexplored parts of your market are where growth lives.
**Action:** All growth is in unmapped territory.
**Principle:** Stay at the edges.

### Estrategias que Sangran
**When:** Your strategy requires constant feeding to work.
**Action:** Some strategies are too expensive to maintain.
**Principle:** Sustainable beats intense.

### Multiplicadores que Atan
**When:** Your leverage is creating obligation instead of freedom.
**Action:** Not all leverage multiplies equally.
**Principle:** Some multiplication is actually multiplication. Some is debt.

---

**Master principle:** VELOCITY isn't about moving fast. It's about moving *right*.
